Yes. I offer secure remote support services for Windows, Microsoft 365, Azure, firewalls, servers, and network systems. Whether you're a small business or enterprise, I ensure timely, professional, and discreet troubleshooting.

Absolutely. I’ve led complete Azure transformations including tenant setup, virtual networks, Active Directory sync, VM deployments, BCDR (Business Continuity & Disaster Recovery), and cost optimization planning.

I provide end-to-end cybersecurity services: Fortinet firewall configuration, vulnerability assessment, MFA and access control, endpoint protection, data loss prevention, and cloud security best practices in Azure and Microsoft 365 environments.

Yes. I have extensive experience in setting up, migrating, and managing Office 365 tenants — including Exchange Online, Teams, SharePoint, OneDrive, and identity/access policies.

❓ Are you available for freelance or contract-based projects?

Yes, I’m open to both short-term and long-term consulting or freelance roles — remote or hybrid. I also support project-based implementation (e.g., firewall rollout, migration, DR planning) as per client needs.

❓ What certifications do you hold?

I hold globally recognized certifications including Microsoft Certified: Azure Administrator Associate, Microsoft Certified IT Professional (MCITP), Cisco Certified Network Associate (CCNA), and Red Hat Certified System Administrator (RHCSA).
